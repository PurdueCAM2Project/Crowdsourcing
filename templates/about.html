About: CAM2 Crowdsourcing Team

Crowdsourcing Team of Purdue CAM2 Research Group was established in 2019 Spring semester, dedicate to find the crowdsourcing approaches for biased image database problem.

Team member: Ming Yin (professor), Xiao Hu(team leader), Haobo Wang, Gore Kao, Altug Gemalmaz, and Pranjali Raturi.

Research focus: Detect and report the biased attributes in the image datasets by utilizing crowdsourcing.
